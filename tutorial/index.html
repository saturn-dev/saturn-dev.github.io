TUTORIAL

everything you need to know before using Kakobuy.

1

COUPONS

Sign up using the button below. Enter your email and create a strong password you’ll remember. After signing up, you’ll receive $400+ USD in shipping coupons!

FREE COUPONS
2

Language & Currency

After signing up, go to the top bar and open settings. Change the language to your preferred language and set the currency to your preferred currency, then click Confirm.

1

Browse Products

After you’ve found the product you want from the spreadsheet, click on Buy on Kakobuy. Make sure to review the product details, like the qc photos and price, before proceeding to ensure everything is correct for your order.

2

Purchasing

When you find a product you like, select the options (size, color, etc.) and click buy now or add to cart. Once wanting to purchase click buy now and select what payment method (most popular; paypal, apple pay, google pay, etc).

3

Seller Shipping

Most sellers ship in about 2–3 days, but always check if the item is a preorder. Some sellers ship overnight. Once it arrives at the warehouse, you’ll get free QC photos of your item.

4

Warehouse

Once your product arrives, you’ll receive free QC (quality check) photos. You can then choose to keep the item or request a return/exchange within a 5-day window. In the meantime, KakoBuy will store your product for free.

1

Creating Parcel

Once all of your products are stored in the warehouse, you can create a parcel. From there, you can select all the products you want to include in that shipment. For value-added services, I would use moister-barrier bag, and corner protection.

2

Shipping Lines USA

I use UPS (tax extra) for fast delivery in 8–10 days, but it requires a 45% tariff deposit (for usa). Duty-Free Air is my recommendation—tax-free and takes 2–3 weeks, much cheaper. Cargo Boat takes over 4 months, but is the cheapest option. And about seizing mainly in the USA it is very very rare to get seized, I personally never seen it happen to anyone, you don't need to worry.

3

Tracking parcel / delivery

Once your parcel is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number. Enter it on 17track.net for faster updates. When your parcel arrives, feel free to share your in-hand haul on the Discord!
